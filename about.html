All right, this is Gamefi Calculator!

Gamefi is a new trend not only for the games industry but also for the crypto economy. The biggest question when entering one of these games is: how much time will it take for you to earn your initial investment back?

With this in mind, this website is made to show the potential earnings of the 2 biggest games in the Gamefi industry, to help people who want to know how much they would make in certain periods of time.
